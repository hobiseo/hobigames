Teen Patti AK47 Variation

The name comes from the well-known “AK47 rifle.”

The jokers or wild cards in this 3-patti version are the A(♠♥♦♣), K(♠♥♦♣), 4(♠♥♦♣), and 7(♠♥♦♣) of all suits.
The game will be won by the player having the best three-patti hand. Other regulations and gaming remain the same.

Example  1

Assuming we receive A♠-K♦-4♠, this will result in A-A- A. (as all three cards Ace, King, 4 are jokers)

Example 2

For instance, if we have A♠-2♠-2♥ while the other team obtains Q♥-4♥-7♣, the other team will succeed.

(Q-4- 7 = Q-Q- Q because the jokers are 4 & 7, and we have 2-2- 2)
